Project Title

Lumiere Home

Client

College Project

Description

Lumiere Home is a Homeless shelter located in the city of Ottawa. It was founded in the year 2020 by two young women who realized during the heat of the pandemic, that while the government kept emphasizing for people to stay home to curb the spread of the virus, many young people were without roofs over their heads. The founders started by opening up their homes to young people who needed a place to stay and food in their bellies. Now in 2021, Lumiere Home has a government approved facility for homeless young people within the ages of 18-35, to get shelter and food especially during the pandemic. They also provide training for these young people and equip them with certain skills that could help them in getting jobs within the society or help them enroll in schools.
